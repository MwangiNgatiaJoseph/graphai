## Using GraphAI on Mobile

### Uploading a Graph

1. **Open GraphAI** from your home screen
2. **Enter Graph Title** - e.g., "EUR/USD 4H Chart"
3. **Add Description** (optional) - e.g., "Looking for breakout signals"
4. **Choose Upload Method**:
   - **Tap upload area** to select from your photo library
   - **Tap "Capture with Camera"** to photograph a chart directly
5. **Review the image** and tap "Use This Photo" if capturing
6. **Tap "Start Analysis"** to begin

### Asking Questions

1. **Type your question** in the message box
2. **Tap Send** (arrow button)
3. **Wait for AI response** - typically 2-5 seconds
4. **Ask follow-up questions** - context is maintained
5. **Scroll up** to see previous messages

### Managing Sessions

1. **View Previous Sessions** - Sidebar shows all your past analyses
2. **Tap a session** to reload it and continue the conversation
3. **Start New Session** - Go back to upload screen to analyze a new graph
4. **Admin Panel** (Creator only) - Tap "Admin Panel" to view all sessions

## Troubleshooting

### Camera Not Working
- **Check Permissions**: Go to Settings → Apps → GraphAI → Permissions → Camera
- **Restart App**: Close and reopen GraphAI
- **Clear Cache**: Go to Settings → Apps → GraphAI → Storage → Clear Cache
- **Try Browser**: If PWA camera fails, use your browser's camera app

### App Not Installing
- **Update Browser**: Make sure Chrome/Safari is up to date
- **Check Storage**: Ensure you have at least 50MB free space
- **Clear Cache**: Clear browser cache and try again
- **Try Different Browser**: If one browser fails, try another

### Offline Issues
- **Check Connection**: Verify you have internet when first loading
- **Refresh Page**: Pull down to refresh and sync data
- **Clear Service Worker**: Settings → Apps → GraphAI → Storage → Clear All Data

### Slow Performance
- **Close Other Apps**: Free up RAM by closing background apps
- **Clear Cache**: Go to Settings → Apps → GraphAI → Storage → Clear Cache
- **Restart Phone**: Restart your device for better performance
- **Check Internet**: Ensure you have a stable connection

## FAQ

**Q: Can I use GraphAI offline?**
A: Partially. You can view previous sessions offline, but new uploads and AI responses require internet.

**Q: How much data does it use?**
A: Approximately 2-5 MB per session, depending on image size and conversation length.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
